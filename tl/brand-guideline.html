The copyright license does not include a license to use our trademark, and this Policy is intended to explain how to use our marks consistent with background law and design expectations.

Our word trademarks and service marks: dApploy Our logos: The dApploy logos (and all visual derivatives) Other assets both visual or copy produced by dApploy including but not limited to illustrations and catchphrases.
